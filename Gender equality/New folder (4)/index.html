Gender Equality

What is Gender Equality?

Gender equality is the idea of both genders which is male
and female to equally enjoy their rights as human beings.
Gender equality pertains practicing these rights without
discriminating genders or being discriminated by genders.

About Us

The purpose of our website is to promote GENDER EQUALITY amongst the youth and adults.
Saint Aloysius Gonzaga Gender Equality website enlightens people to see what it is all
about the Gender Equality. We aim to reduce and if possible remove the discrimination of genders.
