Advocacy

The Advocacy program of the Saint Aloysius Gonzaga Parish is aimed to let people of the same gender
expand their thinking in what is gender equality. it is not teaching women to be like men but teaching women
to take on roles that they CAN do. it is not teaching women to stop doing house duties and be employed.

It is not teaching men that they can only do the work and they cannot do the house duties.
It is teaching men that house duties and employment is a shared responsibility.
We do these duties to ensure that men and women are aware about GENDER EQUALITY.
